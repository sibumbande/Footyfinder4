About Us

At Footy Finder , we are passionate about revolutionizing how people engage with amateur sports. From providing a platform for players to showcase their skills to creating a unique soccer culture in South Africa , our mission is to blend sports, technology, and community for something truly special.

Our team consists of sports enthusiasts and tech innovators dedicated to creating a seamless, engaging experience for players and fans alike. We believe that the power of blockchain can bring transparency, inclusivity, and excitement to grassroots sports in South Africa and beyond.
